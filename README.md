# AI Prompt Engineering Portfolio

## Project Overview

This project presents an AI-powered assistant for automating LinkedIn content creation and publishing.

The assistant transforms a routine marketing workflow into a structured semi-automated process. It supports idea collection, LinkedIn post generation, visual creation, publication, and status tracking.

## Business Problem

Content managers and marketers often spend a lot of time preparing LinkedIn content manually. This includes generating ideas, writing posts, creating visuals, selecting hashtags, adding calls to action, and planning publications.

Manual content production may lead to:

- high time costs;
- inconsistent posting frequency;
- unstable content quality;
- repetitive work;
- errors and missed steps;
- difficulty maintaining a consistent tone of voice and CTA structure.

## Solution

The proposed solution is an AI assistant that automates the LinkedIn content workflow.

The workflow follows this logic:

Idea → Post Text → Visual → Publication → Status Tracking

The system helps generate LinkedIn posts, create visual assets, manage content ideas, track statuses, and support publication through automation tools.

## Tools and Technologies

- Telegram Bot
- Zapier
- Google Sheets
- OpenAI GPT
- DALL·E
- LinkedIn integration

## Workflow

1. The user submits a content idea through Telegram.
2. The idea is saved and tracked in Google Sheets.
3. OpenAI GPT generates a LinkedIn post based on the idea and content requirements.
4. DALL·E generates a visual asset for the post.
5. Zapier connects the tools and manages the automation workflow.
6. The post is published on LinkedIn.
7. The publication status and post URL are saved in Google Sheets.

## Prompt Engineering Component

The project demonstrates the use of prompt engineering for marketing content generation.

The prompts are designed to support:

- clear post structure;
- consistent tone of voice;
- relevant calls to action;
- hashtag generation;
- audience-oriented messaging;
- adaptation of raw ideas into professional LinkedIn posts.

## Business Value

The project shows how AI and low-code automation can reduce manual work and improve the stability of content production.

Expected benefits include:

- saving time on routine content creation;
- reducing manual workload;
- improving consistency of LinkedIn communication;
- supporting regular publication;
- increasing productivity;
- enabling faster content experimentation.

## Expected Results

According to the project concept, the AI assistant can help reduce manual content management work and support a more efficient LinkedIn content process.

The expected impact includes:

- approximately 124 hours saved per month;
- estimated monthly cost reduction of $2,480;
- productivity growth of approximately 60%.
